SkyUI

ABOUT THIS MOD:

Elegant, PC-friendly interface mod with many advanced features./

SkyUI is an elegant, PC-friendly UI mod for Skyrim with many advanced features. All improvements seamlessly integrate with the style of the original interface. Version 5 includes better crafting, enchanting, alchemy and smithing menus and several other enhancements.

Features include:
1.)Convenient inventory management with a feature-rich panel that supports full-text search, sorting and extended data columns

2.)Version 5 extends this to all menus, including crafting, enchanting, alchemy and smithing.

3.)A powerful, categorized favorites menu that still keeps true to the original idea of a fast, non-intrusive way to access your essential items. Eight customizable hotkey groups allow to explore playstyles that were way too tedious before.

4.)The Mod Configuration Menu (MCM) to support user-friendly in-game customization. It's not just used by SkyUI, but by hundreds of other mods as well.

5.)Full-text search and highlighting of discovered locations on the map.

6.)Active effects are displayed directly in the HUD, instead of having to constantly open the magic menu. Highly customizable.

7.)Gamepad support, and intuitive and efficient keyboard navigation.

8.)Multi-language support for English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Russian, Polish, Czech and Japanese.

Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch

ABOUT THIS MOD:

A comprehensive bugfixing mod for The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim - Legendary Edition. The goal of the Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch (aka USLEEP) is to eventually fix every bug with Skyrim and its 3 DLCs not officially resolved by the developers to the limits of the Creation Kit and community-developed tools, in one easy-to-install package.

Features:
- Hundreds of gameplay, quest, NPC, object, item, text and placement bugs fixed.
- Created by the authors of the Unofficial Oblivion Patch series.
- No unsafe changes (such as deletions of stock objects).
- Designed to be compatible with as many other mods as possible.

REQUIREMENTS

Official Skyrim patch 1.9.32.0.8 or greater.
Official Dawnguard DLC.
Official Hearthfire DLC.
Official Dragonborn DLC.
